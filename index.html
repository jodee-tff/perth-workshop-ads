01 / Campaign Structure

Two-Campaign Architecture

Use Ad Set Budget Optimization (ABO), not CBO. With under $2,000 total spend, CBO starves smaller ad sets. Meta needs ~50 conversions per ad set per week to exit learning phase — unreachable at this budget with CBO spreading spend.

Campaign 1 — Registrations (75% of budget / ~$1,365)

Objective: Leads (Instant Form) or Conversions (website registration)

Ad SetAudienceDaily BudgetDuration

After Day 14, shut down Campaign 2 and redistribute all budget to Campaign 1.

Bidding Strategy

  • Lowest Cost (automatic bidding) across all ad sets — no cost caps or bid caps
  • Optimise for maximum number of conversions (not value)
  • Attribution window: 7-day click, 1-day view
  • Do NOT use Traffic objective — it optimises for clicks from low-intent users

The First 3 Seconds — Three Proven Approaches

A: Pattern Interrupt

Black screen with large white text: "STOP using AI like a beginner." Speaker walks into frame.

B: Mid-Sentence Energy

Speaker already in frame, animated: "...and that's why 90% of business owners are using AI completely wrong."

C: Screen Recording

AI tool doing something impressive at 2x speed. Text: "This took 3 minutes. It used to take 3 hours." Cut to speaker.

Production Notes

  • Shoot vertically (9:16) for Reels and Stories
  • Film in natural light — UGC style outperforms polished by 2–3x on CTR
  • Always add captions — 85% of video watched without sound
  • Audio quality > video quality — use a lapel mic
  • Background: real office, co-working space, or Perth landmark
  • Speaker close to camera — fills the frame, feels personal

07 / Positioning

Positioning AI for Business Audiences

Core principle: Frame AI as a business tool, not a technology. The audience cares about time, money, competitive advantage — not the tech itself.

Language Guide

Artificial intelligence→AI tools / AI systems
Machine learning models→Smart workflows
Prompt engineering→Getting better results from AI
Large language models→AI assistants
Fine-tuning→Customising AI for your business
API integration→Making your tools work together
Technical implementation→Hands-on setup
Cutting-edge technology→Practical tools available right now

Five Positioning Angles

1. Productivity

"What if your entire team could get an extra day back every week?"

2. Competitive Advantage

"Your competitors are already using these tools. Are you going to lead or catch up?"

3. Demystification

"You've heard the hype. This workshop shows you what actually works."

4. Practical Implementation

"You'll build real AI systems and walk out with tools you'll use immediately."

5. Leadership

"Your team is waiting for someone to lead them into AI. That's you."

Perth-Specific

"World-class AI training — no flight to Sydney required." Tap into local pride. Reference mining, resources, professional services.

09 / Pricing Strategy

Early Bird & Pricing

Do NOT show the full price in the ad. Drive to the landing page and let it build value first.

TimelinePricing TacticMessaging

11 / Tracking & Decision Rules

Tracking & Measurement

Day 1 Setup Checklist

  • Meta Pixel installed on all landing/registration pages
  • Custom conversion created for registration confirmation page
  • Conversions API (CAPI) configured if platform supports it
  • UTM parameters on all ad links
  • Custom Audiences created: email list, website visitors, page engagers
  • Conversion exclusion audience: people who completed registration

Daily Monitoring Targets
