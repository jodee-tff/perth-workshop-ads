## Placement Notes

- All ads should run across **Advantage+ Placements** (let Meta optimise).
- Videos are shot 9:16 for Reels/Stories. Meta will auto-adapt for Feed.
- Static images: provide both 1:1 (Feed) and 9:16 (Stories/Reels) versions where possible.
- All video must have **burnt-in captions** — 85% of video on Meta is watched without sound.
- Ensure the **landing page URL** is consistent across all ads and includes UTM parameters:
  `?utm_source=meta&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=perth-ai-workshop&utm_content=[ad-name]`
